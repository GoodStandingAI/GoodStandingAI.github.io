Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

How does the pay-as-you-go model work?
With our PAYG model, you only pay for the services you actually use each month. There are no minimums, upfront costs, or hidden fees. You get billed monthly in arrears based on your actual usage.
What are the benefits of bundled plans?
Bundled plans provide cost certainty with predictable monthly billing while including generous credits for various services. They're ideal for firms that prefer budgeting predictability and often provide cost savings for regular users.
Can I set spending limits to control costs?
Yes! You can set spending limits to control monthly usage costs. If your limit is reached, the service will automatically stop usage for that month, preventing any further charges.
What happens if I exceed my bundled plan limits?
Any overages on bundled plans are billed at the same pay-as-you-go rates. This ensures you always have access to services when needed while maintaining fair, transparent pricing.
Is our data secure and compliant?
Yes. GoodStandingAI uses enterprise-grade security with bank-level encryption. All data is stored on Canadian servers to meet privacy requirements and follows industry best practices for legal technology.
Can I change or cancel plans easily?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade anytime between bundled plans or switch to pure pay-as-you-go. No long-term contracts required.

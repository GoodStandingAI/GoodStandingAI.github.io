Performance-Optimized Hero Sections

Our Hero component implements industry best practices for performance:

🚀 Lazy Loading

Below-the-fold heroes use Intersection Observer for lazy loading, improving initial page load speed.

📱 Responsive Images

Uses srcset and modern formats (WebP/AVIF) with intelligent sizing based on viewport.

♿ Accessibility

Full keyboard navigation, ARIA labels, focus management, and screen reader support.

🎬 Video Support

Optimized video backgrounds with poster images and multiple format support.

🎨 Apple Design

Clean, minimal design with smooth animations and high-quality visual hierarchy.

⚡ Priority Control

Above-the-fold heroes load immediately while below-the-fold content is lazy-loaded.

About GoodStanding AI

A Canadian legal-tech startup focused on modernizing corporate records management for law firms and their clients.

Gone are the days of dusty minute books and frantic last-minute filings. Our platform delivers a modern, AI-driven solution that keeps corporate records at your fingertips and ensures your clients are always in good standing. In short, we provide not just software, but peace-of-mind compliance, efficient workflows, and the confidence that your firm's companies will always remain compliant.

We combine a compliance-first mindset with cutting-edge technology to replace archaic manual processes with a secure, intuitive system designed for today's law practices.

Our Mission

At GoodStanding AI, our mission is to bring peace-of-mind compliance to corporate law firms by making entity record-keeping effortless. We strive to keep every client's company compliant and confident, eliminating last-minute scrambles or missed filings.

We achieve this by transforming messy, manual corporate record work into a single automated, cloud-based source of truth, ensuring every entity remains up-to-date without the fire drills. In essence, we're here to keep you always in good standing through modern efficiency and thoughtful innovation.

Our Journey

Our story highlights a commitment to solving compliance challenges with modern tech

Foundation & Vision

June 2025: GoodStanding AI is founded in Alberta, Canada, and the vision takes shape. We begin an intensive research and planning phase – crafting our business plan, refining the product roadmap, and engaging with law firms to pinpoint their needs.

MVP Development

September 2025: Development of our first MVP (Minimum Viable Product) begins. After four weeks of planning, we start building the core platform (while keeping things lean and focused) to bring our compliance solution to life.

Alpha & Beta Testing

December 2025: Early alpha and beta testing kicks off with a select group of Alberta law firms. Firms join our pilot programs, using GoodStanding AI in real scenarios. Their feedback helps us refine key features – like the Annual Return Agent and Document Generation tools – ensuring the product truly meets real-world needs.

Official Launch

January 2026: Official launch of GoodStanding AI. Armed with months of testing and iteration, we open up the platform to law firms everywhere. This launch marks the beginning of our wider mission to modernize compliance management for firms across Canada and beyond.

Clean Room Development: Throughout our journey, we remained committed to a "clean room" development approach – building our software from the ground up to ensure originality and legal IP cleanliness. This careful foundation means our customers can trust the integrity and security of our platform.

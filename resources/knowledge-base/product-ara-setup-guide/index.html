What is the Annual Return Agent (ARA)?

The Annual Return Agent (ARA) is GoodStandingAI's automated compliance system that monitors, prepares, and files annual returns for your corporate portfolio across all Canadian jurisdictions. This intelligent system eliminates the manual work of tracking due dates, preparing forms, and ensuring timely filings, while providing complete transparency and control over the process.

ARA works as your dedicated compliance assistant, continuously monitoring your corporate portfolio for upcoming annual return deadlines and automatically handling the entire filing process from start to finish. The system integrates seamlessly with your digital minute books and compliance monitoring systems to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

How ARA Works

The Annual Return Agent operates on a sophisticated monitoring and filing system that tracks each corporation's specific requirements based on its jurisdiction, incorporation date, and filing history. The system automatically calculates due dates, prepares accurate returns using current corporate information, and files them with the appropriate government registry.

For each corporation in your portfolio, ARA maintains a real-time compliance calendar that accounts for jurisdiction-specific rules, holiday adjustments, and penalty deadlines. When a filing becomes due, the system automatically pulls the most current corporate information from your minute book and prepares the required forms with 100% accuracy.

Comprehensive Jurisdiction Coverage

ARA supports annual return filing for all Canadian corporate jurisdictions, including federal corporations (CBCA) and all provincial jurisdictions. The system automatically adapts to each jurisdiction's specific requirements, forms, deadlines, and fee structures, ensuring compliance regardless of where your corporations are incorporated.

  • Federal (CBCA): Corporations Canada annual returns and updates
  • Alberta (ABCA): Alberta Corporate Registry annual returns
  • Ontario (OBCA): ServiceOntario annual returns
  • British Columbia (BCBCA): BC Registry Services annual reports
  • Other Provinces: Complete coverage for all remaining Canadian jurisdictions

Step 2: Filing Preferences Configuration

ARA offers flexible filing preferences to match your firm's workflow and client requirements. You can configure different automation levels for different clients or corporation types, ensuring the system works exactly how you need it to.

Automation Level Options

Fully Automated Filing

Recommended for routine filings. ARA automatically prepares and files annual returns without requiring approval, using current corporate information from your minute books.

✓ Best for: Established corporations with stable information and regular filing patterns

Review & Approve Mode

ARA prepares annual returns and sends them for your review and approval before filing. You receive a complete preview with the option to make adjustments if needed.

✓ Best for: Complex corporations, recent changes, or clients requiring manual oversight

Notification Only

ARA monitors deadlines and sends notifications but doesn't prepare or file returns. You handle the actual filing using traditional methods.

✓ Best for: Transitioning to automation or corporations requiring special handling

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does ARA setup take for a typical portfolio?

Most practices complete ARA setup in 15-30 minutes for portfolios up to 50 corporations. Larger portfolios with bulk import can be configured in under an hour. Our setup wizard guides you through each step with validation to ensure accuracy.

What happens if my corporation information changes after setup?

ARA integrates with your digital minute books and automatically detects changes. When integrated with our Minute Book Health Monitor, any corporate changes are reflected in ARA immediately. You can also manually update information or set ARA to require approval for filings when changes are detected.

Can I test ARA before activating automated filing?

Yes! ARA includes a comprehensive testing mode where you can verify all settings, review prepared returns, and test notifications without actually filing. You can also start with "Review & Approve" mode to manually approve each filing until you're comfortable with full automation.

What if ARA files an incorrect return?

ARA has a 99.9% accuracy rate due to direct registry integration and data validation. In the rare case of an error, our compliance team assists with corrections at no additional cost. We also provide filing insurance coverage and maintain detailed audit trails for all submissions.

Does ARA support all Canadian jurisdictions?

Yes, ARA supports federal corporations (CBCA) and all provincial jurisdictions including Alberta, Ontario, British Columbia, and all other provinces. Each jurisdiction's specific requirements, forms, and deadlines are automatically handled.

How does ARA pricing compare to manual filing costs?

ARA costs $15 per filing compared to $150+ in typical billable time for manual filing. Including penalty avoidance and administrative savings, most practices save $135+ per filing. The system pays for itself after the first few automated filings.

Can I configure different settings for different clients?

Absolutely. ARA allows corporation-specific or client-specific settings including automation levels, notification preferences, payment methods, and approval workflows. This flexibility ensures the system works exactly how each client prefers.

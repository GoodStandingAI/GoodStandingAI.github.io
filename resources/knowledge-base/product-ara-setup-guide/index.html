What is the Annual Return Agent (ARA)?

The Annual Return Agent (ARA) is GoodStandingAI's automated compliance system that monitors, prepares, and submits annual returns for your corporate portfolio across multiple jurisdictions. This system reduces the manual work of tracking due dates and preparing forms, while providing transparency and control over the process.

ARA works as your dedicated compliance assistant, continuously monitoring your corporate portfolio for upcoming annual return deadlines and automatically handling the entire filing process from start to finish. The system integrates seamlessly with your digital minute books and compliance monitoring systems to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

How ARA Works

The Annual Return Agent tracks each corporation's annual return requirements based on its jurisdiction and key details. It prepares returns using current corporate information and supports a streamlined filing workflow based on your preferred level of oversight.

For each corporation in your portfolio, ARA maintains a compliance calendar for annual return obligations. When a filing is coming up, the system uses current corporate information from your minute book to prepare what you needâ€”then you proceed based on your preferred level of oversight.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does ARA setup take for a typical portfolio?

Setup time varies by portfolio size and preferences. ARA provides guided setup to help you configure key settings efficiently.

What happens if my corporation information changes after setup?

ARA uses your stored corporate information for filings, and updates can be reflected in future filing preparation. You can review and adjust information as needed before submission.

Can I test ARA before activating automated filing?

Yes. You can start with a review-first workflow to validate settings and review prepared filings before enabling deeper automation.

What if ARA files an incorrect return?

ARA includes validation and detailed audit trails. In the rare case of an issue, our team can assist with corrections and next steps.

Does ARA support multiple jurisdictions?

ARA is built for Canadian corporate compliance with support for multiple jurisdictions and their requirements. Coverage can vary, so please contact us to confirm availability for your specific jurisdiction.

How does ARA pricing compare to manual filing costs?

ARA is priced to be cost-effective relative to manual processing, with savings driven by reduced administrative work and fewer last-minute issues.

Can I configure different settings for different clients?

Absolutely. ARA allows corporation-specific or client-specific settings including automation levels, notification preferences, payment methods, and approval workflows. This flexibility ensures the system works exactly how each client prefers.

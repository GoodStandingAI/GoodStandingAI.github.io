Getting Started with AI Document Automation

1. Start with Templates

Begin by creating AI-assisted templates for your most common documents. The AI learns your organization's style, terminology, and legal requirements over time, improving the quality of generated documents.

2. Define Your Parameters

Set up your corporate information, standard clauses, and preferred language. Include your jurisdiction's specific requirements, your corporation's structure, and any unique business needs.

3. Train the System

Upload examples of your existing documents to help the AI understand your preferences. Review and refine generated documents to improve future outputs.

4. Implement Workflows

Create automated workflows that trigger document generation based on specific events or deadlines. Set up approval processes and version control for generated documents.

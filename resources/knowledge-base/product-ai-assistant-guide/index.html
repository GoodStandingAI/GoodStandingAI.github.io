AI-Powered Compliance Assistant

GoodStandingAI's AI Assistant for Compliance Questions (AIPA) is a built-in helper that uses artificial intelligence to answer your corporate compliance questions quickly and easily. As a cutting-edge feature, GoodStandingAI is among the first platforms to embed a practical AI assistant that can auto-draft routine documents and answer compliance questions with ease.

In everyday terms, AIPA acts like an on-demand paralegal – you simply ask it a question about your company's records or filings, and it will fetch the answer for you in seconds. This guide explains what AIPA can do and how to use it, in clear language for first-time users.

  • • Get instant answers to compliance questions in plain English
  • • Search and summarize your corporate records automatically
  • • Receive step-by-step guidance for complex procedures
  • • Draft routine documents with AI assistance
  • • Monitor compliance status across multiple entities

What Can the AI Assistant Do?

AIPA leverages GoodStandingAI's advanced engine for smart search and assistance. It indexes all your uploaded corporate records and minute book documents for natural-language queries, allowing you to ask questions in plain English and quickly retrieve answers or relevant documents. In other words, you don't need to remember exact file names or navigate through folders – just ask, and the assistant will find the information for you.

For example, you can ask a direct question like "Is Company X missing any annual filings?" and get an instant answer about that entity's compliance status. AIPA is knowledgeable about common Canadian corporate compliance requirements, so it understands queries about annual returns, director updates, registered offices, and other obligations applicable across jurisdictions in Canada.

How to Use the AI Assistant (Step by Step)

Using AIPA is straightforward and requires no special setup or technical expertise. Follow these steps to get started with the AI compliance assistant:

📱 Step-by-Step Usage Guide

1. Open the Assistant

In the GoodStandingAI application, locate the AI Assistant (AIPA) icon or chat window. This is usually accessible from the dashboard or within an entity's record page. Look for a chat bubble icon labeled "Ask AIPA" or similar in the interface.

2. Ask Your Question

Click the assistant and simply type your question in the text box. You can phrase questions naturally, as if you were asking a colleague. For example: "Is our ABC Corp annual return filed for this year?" or "List all upcoming deadlines for my companies." You have flexibility in how you ask – AIPA understands everyday language, so you don't need to use specific commands or jargon.

3. Review the Answer

Press Enter or send your question. AIPA will process your query and in seconds present an answer on the chat screen. The response will typically include the key information you asked for – such as a yes/no on a filing status, a date, or a short summary pulled from your records. It may also provide additional context or links.

4. Follow Up or Refine

If you need more details, you can ask a follow-up question in the same chat. AIPA remembers the context of your conversation. So after the above answer, you could continue with, "What happens if we miss that deadline?" and the assistant will explain the implications or late-filing penalties, if known.

5. Take Action (if needed)

Often, AIPA's answer will be all you need. But if the assistant's response indicates an issue (for example, a missed filing) or you asked for procedure guidance, it might suggest a next step. It could provide a link or button to the relevant form or GoodStandingAI feature.

💬 Tips for Using AIPA

  • • You can use AIPA at any time – whether you're in the middle of updating a record or just doing a quick check-in on compliance statuses
  • • Feel free to ask questions across different entities or topics
  • • If you have multiple companies, it's a good practice to mention the company name in your question for clarity (e.g., "Does Acme Corp have any outstanding filings?")
  • • If you forget to specify and the query is ambiguous, AIPA may ask you to clarify which company or record you mean

Getting the Most Out of AIPA

To maximize the benefits of the AI assistant, keep these best practices in mind:

  • Ask in Plain Language: There's no need for formal legal phrasing or complex search syntax. AIPA is designed to understand natural language questions. Writing as you would speak (e.g., "Show me the last annual report for 2025") is usually the most effective way to query the system.
  • Be Specific When Possible: While the assistant can handle broad questions, being specific will yield more precise answers. Include names of entities or document types if you know them. For instance, "Does 123 Investments Ltd. have a Minute Book Health Check report?" will get a direct answer about that specific company.
  • Leverage Context in Chat: You can build on previous questions. This means you don't have to repeat full context every time. If you ask, "When was XYZ Corp's last annual return filed?" and then follow up with "Was it on time?", AIPA knows "it" refers to that filing.
  • Use It for Learning: AIPA isn't just for retrieving facts – it's also a learning tool. If you're unsure about a compliance requirement or GoodStandingAI feature, you can ask the assistant for an explanation. For example, "What is a Minute Book Health Check?" will prompt AIPA to give a brief explanation of that feature or concept.
  • Trust but Verify Critical Info: The AI assistant uses GoodStandingAI's data and the latest in generative AI technology to provide answers, with careful measures in place to ensure accuracy and confidentiality. Still, for critical filings or legal decisions, it's good practice to double-check important details.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AIPA and how does it help with compliance?

AIPA (AI Assistant for Compliance Questions) is GoodStandingAI's built-in AI assistant that uses artificial intelligence to answer corporate compliance questions instantly. It acts like an on-demand paralegal, searching your corporate records and providing answers about filings, deadlines, and compliance requirements in seconds.

What types of questions can I ask AIPA?

You can ask AIPA about compliance status checks (filing deadlines, overdue items), entity information (directors, registered office), document searches (meeting minutes, resolutions), and procedural guidance (how to file returns, add directors). AIPA understands natural language questions and can handle queries across multiple entities.
